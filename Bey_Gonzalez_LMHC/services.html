Services:

  • Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder:
    Happens after a person is exposed to a stressful incident
    of any sort, that exceeds their natural ability to cope.
    If left untreated it can lead to depression, anxiety,
    or drug abuse. Individuals may experience symptoms
    and not yet have the disorder.
  • Offered Services:

  • Children
  • A therapeutic approach that allows children to
    express their thoughts and emotions by using
    specific games and toys.

  • Couples
  • Utilizes an emotionally based approach that assists
    couples in learning about negative and positive
    communications, which promotes healthy ways of
    dealing with conflict.

  • Families
  • Families will learn to deal with daily life stressors in a
    positive way, learning to eliminate irrational beliefs from their thinking.

  • Greif
  • Helpful for those that have experienced loss.
    Individuals will learn to deal with divorce,
    death of friends, relatives sometimes even pets.
    Teaching individuals how to return to normal life.

  • Individual
  • Helps people to learn positive coping skills and to live healthy lives

  • Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)
  • Designed to assist an individuals to better understand their disorder and find a way to live beyond it.

  • Life Coaching
  • A technique used to help individuals dispose of
    hindering habits. Helps achieve success in business.
