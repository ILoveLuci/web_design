Bey is a professional counselor, licensed by the Florida Board of Clinical social work, Marriage & Family Therapy and Mental Health Counseling.
He can provide counseling services for:

  • Individuals
  • Couples
  • Families
  • Children
  • Psychosocial assessments
  • Post-traumatic Stress Disorder
  • Grief

I began my counseling practice to assist
others in finding a holistic way to deal with
life stressors. I have been in the healing
business for over 10 years. My journey began
with assisting others in a hospital setting, as a
Spiritual Leader (Chaplain), often bringing
comfort to those in their final days, or
empowering the loved ones left behind.
In those experiences and many more I
developed a love for counseling others.


Bey worked hard to attain his goal of being
licensed by the State of Florida as a Licensed
Mental Health Counselor. He now provides
individual services, family services and
life coaching. His unique style of utilizing
CBT, REBT, Emotional and Spiritually based
counseling has helped many conquer their
fears and problems and bring solutions to their
issues.
